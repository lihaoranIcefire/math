\begin{definition}
$\mathcal D$ is the \textit{test function space}, a \textit{distribution}\index{Distribution} is a functional $T:\mathcal D\to\mathbb R$ such that if $\phi_j\to\phi$ in $\mathcal D$, then $T(\phi_j)=T(\phi)$
\end{definition}
