  1. Find the length of the curve parametrized by \[\mathbf r(t)=(t\sin t+\cos t)\mathbf i+(-t\cos t+\sin t)\mathbf j+t^2\mathbf k,\qquad0\leq t\leq 2 \] First we see \[\mathbf r'(t)=(\sin t+t\cos t-\sin t)\mathbf i+(-\cos t+t\sin t+\cos t)\mathbf j+2t\mathbf k=t\cos t\mathbf i+t\sin t\mathbf j+2t\mathbf k \] so \[\|\mathbf r'(t)\|=\sqrt{(t\cos t)^2+(t\sin t)^2+(2t)^2}=\sqrt{5t^2}=\sqrt5|t| \] Therefore the length of the curve is \[L=\int_0^2\|\mathbf r'(t)\|dt=\int_0^2\sqrt5|t|dt=\int_0^2\sqrt5tdt=2\sqrt5 \]
