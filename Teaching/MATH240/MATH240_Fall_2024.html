First we get the augmented matrix of the linear system

\[\begin{cases} 3x_1+ 6x_2-3x_3=6\\ -2x_1-4x_2-3x_3=-1\\ 3x_1+ 6x_2-2x_3=10 \end{cases} \]

which is

\[\begin{align*} &\begin{bmatrix} 3&6&-3&6\\ -2&-4&-3&-1\\ 3&6&-2&10\\ \end{bmatrix} \xrightarrow{(\frac{1}{3})R_1} \begin{bmatrix} 1&2&-1&2\\ -2&-4&-3&-1\\ 3&6&-2&10\\ \end{bmatrix} \xrightarrow{\substack{2R_1+R_2\\(-3)R_1+R_2}} \begin{bmatrix} 1&2&-1&2\\ 0&0&-5&3\\ 0&0&1&4\\ \end{bmatrix}\\ &\xrightarrow{5R_3+R_2} \begin{bmatrix} 1&2&-1&2\\ 0&0&0&23\\ 0&0&1&4\\ \end{bmatrix} \end{align*} \]

Notice that \(R_2\) reads

\[0=0x_1+0x_2+0x_3=23 \]

which is impossible, therefore the original linear system has no solutions
