About me

I am Haoran Li(李浩然), or Harrison Li, as students like to call me, I am currently a sixth-year PhD student in the math department at university of Maryland. My advisor is Christian Zickert. My area of research focuses on polylogarithms and their applications within number theory. Before coming to the United States, I went to Sun Yat-sen university and got my Bachelor's degree in mathematics.

Over the past few years, I have been greatly inspired by the advancements in artificial intelligence, igniting a passion to apply my mathematical expertise to real-world problems.
